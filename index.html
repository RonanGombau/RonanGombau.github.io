About Us

Welcome to Havstrøm, your trusted partner for sailboat repairs and electric engine conversions in Northern Norway. Based in the breathtaking island of Senja, we specialize in transforming traditional sailboats into eco-friendly vessels powered by cutting-edge electric technology. Whether you're looking to reduce your environmental impact or need expert maintenance, we're here to help.

Our team of skilled technicians is passionate about the sea and committed to delivering high-quality services. Operating between Harstad, Finnsnes, and Tromsø, we bring our expertise to your doorstep.

Our Services

  • Electric Engine Conversions: Transition to sustainable sailing with our state-of-the-art electric propulsion systems.
  • Fiberglass Repairs: Expert repairs to keep your boat in top condition.
  • General Maintenance: From hull cleaning to engine checks, we cover all your maintenance needs.
  • Custom Upgrades: Enhance your boat's performance and comfort with our custom solutions.

Operating Areas

We proudly serve the following areas:

  • Harstad: Full services available at the marina.
  • Finnsnes: Convenient location for sailboat owners in the region.
  • Tromsø: On-site repairs and consultations available.
